Community Involvment and Interests

MeetIn.Org
I joined MeetIn Portland, the local chapter of MeetIn.org, in 2008. Since then, I've taken on more leadership roles such as event planner, Welcome Wagon leader, Admin team member, and finally Admin team leader. I have been the Admin team leader since the beginning of 2015. I also am helping to organize the yearly celebration taking place this year in Seattle.
Charitable Foundations
I am involved in two different charitable foundations founded by my late father-- The Bruce G. Theater and Music
I grew up participating in the creation of theater and music, and now support both in my community through attendance. I'm a subscriber to the Oregon Symphony, a member at Oregon Shakespeare Festival, and attend dozens of concerts and theater performances a year.
